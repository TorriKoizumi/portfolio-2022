Aloha! I am a multi disciplined graphic designer obsessed with perfecting my personal workflow and style.

I’m a local boy, born and raised in Kaneohe, who is passionate about art and graphic design.

My excitement for design started in high school and it has been growing ever since. I am a recent graduate from the Kapi'olani Community College New Media Arts Program, where I developed my work ethic and creativity.

When I’m not at my desk, I’m sat in the lineup at Sandy Beach, soaking in the sun and relaxing with that beach therapy.
