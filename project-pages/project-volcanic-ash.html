Volcanic Ash is a small business based in New Hampshire, purveyor of a unique small batch hot sauce. While the sauce needed no changes, the brand was looking for an updated identity and website.

The previous logo was fun but didnâ€™t have the versatility needed to span the entire brand. I began by exploring the website to gather their values and interests. It was clear that they had a love for habanero peppers. It is an integral ingredient in their sauce but also their passion. I thought that it should be the focus of the brand itself. The new logo features the profile of a sliced habanero with an erupting volcano replacing the internals of the pepper.

The website was, to be blunt, a mess. It was difficult to navigate with random information thrown about, and the user only saw the product when they reached the bottom of the page. It needed a redesign, not just for aesthetics, but for usability as well. I kept the dark theme but organized the content to be more user friendly. I also added smoke illustrations to add some visual interest.
